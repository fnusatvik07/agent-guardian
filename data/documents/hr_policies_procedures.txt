HR Policies and Procedures Manual

This comprehensive manual outlines all human resources policies and procedures for employees.

EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING:
- New hire orientation within first week of employment
- IT equipment setup and account provisioning
- Benefits enrollment deadline: 30 days from start date
- Initial performance goal setting with direct manager
- Buddy assignment for first 90 days
- Required training completions: Security, Compliance, Company Culture

PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT:
- Quarterly check-ins between employee and manager
- Annual performance reviews conducted in December
- Goal setting process aligned with company objectives
- 360-degree feedback for leadership positions
- Performance improvement plans when necessary
- Career development planning and skill assessments

LEAVE POLICIES:
- Vacation Time: 15 days first year, 20 days after 2 years, 25 days after 5 years
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year, unused days roll over (max 30 days)
- Personal Days: 3 days per year for personal appointments/emergencies  
- Parental Leave: 12 weeks paid for primary caregiver, 6 weeks for secondary
- Bereavement Leave: 5 days for immediate family, 3 days for extended family
- Jury Duty: Paid leave for duration of service

COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS:
- Salary reviews conducted annually in January
- Health insurance: Company pays 80% of premium costs
- Dental and vision insurance available
- 401(k) retirement plan with 4% company match after 1 year
- Life insurance: 2x annual salary provided by company
- Flexible spending accounts for healthcare and dependent care

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT:
- Annual training budget: $2,000 per employee
- Conference attendance with manager approval
- Internal mentorship program available
- Tuition reimbursement up to $5,000 per year for job-related education
- LinkedIn Learning access for all employees
- Cross-departmental shadowing opportunities

WORKPLACE CONDUCT:
- Anti-harassment and discrimination policies strictly enforced
- Open door policy for reporting concerns to HR or management
- Confidential ethics hotline: 1-800-ETH-LINE
- Social media guidelines for representing the company
- Dress code: Business casual, adjust for client meetings
- Punctuality and attendance expectations

EMPLOYEE RECOGNITION:
- Monthly employee spotlight program
- Annual service awards for tenure milestones
- Spot bonuses for exceptional performance
- Team celebration budgets for achieving goals
- Employee referral bonuses: $1,000 for successful hires
- Innovation awards for process improvements
