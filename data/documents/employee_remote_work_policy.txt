Employee Handbook - Remote Work Policy

Our company supports flexible remote work arrangements to promote work-life balance and productivity.

REMOTE WORK ELIGIBILITY:
- Full-time employees after 90 days of employment
- Part-time employees after 6 months 
- Must have satisfactory performance reviews
- Role must be suitable for remote work

REMOTE WORK ARRANGEMENTS:
- Up to 3 days per week remote work with manager approval
- Fully remote work requires VP approval
- Hybrid schedules must include 2 days minimum in office
- Core collaboration hours: 10 AM - 3 PM local time

EQUIPMENT AND SECURITY:
- Company laptop with VPN access required
- Secure internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps)
- Dedicated workspace with privacy for confidential calls
- All company data must remain encrypted
- No public WiFi for accessing company systems

COMMUNICATION REQUIREMENTS:
- Daily check-ins with immediate supervisor
- Participation in all scheduled team meetings
- Response time expectations: email within 4 hours, Slack within 1 hour
- Video conference for all client meetings

PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS:
- Maintain same productivity levels as in-office work
- Complete all assigned tasks by deadlines
- Participate actively in team collaboration
- Attend mandatory in-person meetings and training

For questions about remote work policies, contact HR at hr@company.com or extension 5555.
